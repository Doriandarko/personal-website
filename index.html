Hello, I am Pietro Schirano.
A passionate designer from Italy based in San Francisco.

I enjoy creating clean, simple, pixel-perfect and emotional designs for desktop and mobile usage.

I am passionate about my work and I really love what I do.
I am a tech enthusiast, a Back to the future evangelist and a guitar player.
You can find me in the gym in my spare time. Some could even define me as a funny guy.

Right now I work as UX engineer at Open Table.
Over the past 3 years I have been working on a series of interesting projects.
I designed a succesfull chat app for health-care HipaaChat, worked as a designer at Hubskip one of the coolest startups in Amsterdam ( which now moved to SF) and I completely redesigned the website of one of the biggest Hosting Providers in the Netherlands. I'have done tons of freelance too.

I am the creator of the gamboy skins pack for iPhone, and of some of the most popular free PSDs.
