I am Pietro Schirano. A passionate designer from Italy based in San Francisco.
I enjoy creating clean, simple, pixel-perfect and emotional designs for desktop and mobile usage. Right now I am an UX engineer at Opentable.

I am passionate about my work and I really love what I do.
I am a tech enthusiast, a Back to the future evangelist and a guitar player.
Some could even define me as a funny guy.

I designed a succesfull app for health-care HipaaChat, worked as a designer at Hubskip (now Flyer), a startups that was based in Amsterdam and I completely redesigned the website of one of the biggest Hosting Providers in the Netherlands.
I'have done tons of freelance too.

I am the creator of the gamboy skins pack for iPhone, and of some of the most popular free PSDs.
